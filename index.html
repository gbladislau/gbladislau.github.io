Profile

I graduated in March 2026 with a BSc in Computer Science from the Universidade Federal do Espirito Santo (UFES). My main interests are machine learning, deep learning, and the engineering work required to move models from promising experiments into desirable products.

At Aumo, I focus on deploying AI models and consulting on enterprise use cases, turning ambiguous business constraints into concrete ML solutions. I care about whether a model can operate in the real world, not just whether it performs well in isolation.

In parallel, I work as a researcher at the High Performance Computing Laboratory (Laboratório de Computação de Alto Desempenho - LCAD) at UFES, with emphasis on CNNs, LLMs, and SLMs.

As a beliver in the open-source community, I also contributed to OUMI by building a hyperparameter search module for model training workflows.

Last but not least, I spent two years, as part of the Global Talent Program from AIESEC, in QA automation at Intra2net, working on integration and GUI tests. Outside technology, I enjoy canoeing and studying Japanese.
